NCC Camps

Explore the Prestigious Camps of NCC

Our Featured Camps

All India Vayu Sainik Camp

Distinguished camp for air force training where cadets showcase their skills in aviation and technical events.

All India Vayu Sainik Camp

The premier NCC Air Wing camp conducted at Air Force stations across India. Selected cadets undergo:

  • Intensive training in aeromodelling and aviation
  • Skeet shooting competitions
  • Aircraft recognition tests
  • Flight simulation experiences
  • Technical workshops with IAF personnel

Duration: 10 days | Location: Various Air Force Stations

Eligibility: 'A' Certificate holders with minimum 'B' grade

Republic Day Camp

National camp held to celebrate Republic Day with cadets across India participating in cultural and military displays.

Republic Day Camp

The most prestigious NCC camp held annually in Delhi:

  • Prime Minister's Rally showcasing NCC talents
  • Participation in Republic Day Parade at Rajpath
  • Cultural performances from all states
  • Best Cadet competitions in various disciplines
  • Interaction with senior military officials

Duration: 4 weeks | Location: Delhi Cantonment

Selection: Through rigorous state and national level competitions

Ek Bharat Shrestha Bharat

Program to promote national integration through cultural exchange between cadets from different states.

Ek Bharat Shrestha Bharat

National integration camp featuring:

  • State pairing system for cultural exchange
  • Language learning sessions
  • Traditional art and craft workshops
  • Joint community service projects
  • Cultural night showcasing diverse traditions

Duration: 2 weeks | Location: Various states on rotation

Unique Feature: Cadets stay with buddy from paired state

Mavlankar Camp

A camp focusing on leadership development and physical fitness for senior cadets.

Mavlankar Camp

Advanced leadership camp includes:

  • Obstacle course training
  • Map reading and navigation exercises
  • Command task execution
  • Public speaking workshops
  • 50km route march challenge

Duration: 12 days | Location: NCC Academies

Named After: First Speaker of Lok Sabha, G.V. Mavlankar

MENU Camp

A special training camp for young NCC cadets focusing on basic military and adventure activities.

MENU Camp

Motivation and Enlightenment for New Units camp offers:

  • Basic drill and discipline training
  • Introduction to weapons handling
  • Team-building activities
  • Adventure sports like rappelling
  • NCC history and values orientation

Duration: 7 days | Location: State NCC headquarters

Purpose: Introduce new cadets to NCC fundamentals

Basic Leadership Camp

Fundamental leadership training for cadets to develop command and organizational skills.

Basic Leadership Camp

Foundation for NCC leadership development:

  • Squad drill commands practice
  • Camp planning and execution
  • Basic first aid certification
  • Time management workshops
  • Leadership role rotations

Duration: 10 days | Location: Group Headquarters

Prerequisite: Minimum 6 months in NCC

Advance Leadership Camp

Advanced training for future leaders in the NCC with complex scenarios and challenges.

Advance Leadership Camp

For senior cadets preparing for officer roles:

  • Crisis management simulations
  • Advanced navigation exercises
  • Officer-like qualities assessment
  • Full camp planning and execution
  • Joint operations with other services

Duration: 14 days | Location: NCC Academies

Selection: Based on BLC performance

Trekking Camp

Challenging outdoor camp with trekking expeditions in difficult terrain to build endurance.

Trekking Camp

Adventure-based camp in mountainous regions:

  • High-altitude treks (up to 12,000 ft)
  • Survival skills training
  • Rock climbing and rappelling
  • Night navigation exercises
  • Environmental conservation projects

Duration: 7-10 days | Location: Himalayan regions

Requirement: Medical fitness certificate mandatory
